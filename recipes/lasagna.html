Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that is perfect for any occasion. It's a great dish to make for a crowd, or to enjoy as leftovers throughout the week. This recipe is easy to make and is sure to be a hit with your family and friends.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Minced Beef
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F.
  2. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions.
  3. While the pasta is cooking, brown the minced beef in a large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Once the beef is browned, add the tomato sauce and salt to the skillet and stir to combine.
  5. Once the pasta is cooked, drain it and set it aside.
  6. Spread a thin layer of the beef and tomato sauce mixture in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Place a layer of pasta on top of the beef and tomato sauce mixture.
  8. Repeat the layers until all of the pasta and beef and tomato sauce mixture is used up.
  9. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  10. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and bubbly.
  11. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before serving.
